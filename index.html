Rot13 Decoder & Word Rotator

ROT13 ("rotate by 13 places", sometimes hyphenated ROT-13) is a simple letter substitution cipher that replaces a letter with the letter 13 letters after it in the alphabet. ROT13 is a special case of the Caesar cipher, developed in ancient Rome. This is my rookie implementation. You could search google for better ones. You can find more about Rot13 here
